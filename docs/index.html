Mark 6 Stats
Mark 6 Stats 基於去中心化理念，整合所有紀錄為本地數據模型，提供快速統計查詢、號碼篩選、記錄管理與自動兌獎功能。本應用程式不涉及任何金錢交易，並永久免費使用。
Mark 6 Stats is built on a decentralized concept, integrating all records into a local data model to provide fast statistical queries, number filtering, record management, and automatic prize checking. This application does not involve any monetary transactions and is permanently free to use.
此應用程式使用 Google OAuth 進行登錄，以安全地存取您的帳戶。
This app uses Google OAuth for login to securely access your account.
