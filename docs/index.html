Mark 6 Stats
Mark 6 Stats is built on a decentralized concept, integrating all records into a local data model for statistical queries, number filtering, records management, and automatic prize checking.
This app uses Google OAuth for login to securely access your account.
  1. Tap numbers to mark them as ignored or locked. Press the main button to generate multiple random numbers, choose whether to allow duplicates, and save results for automatic prize checking at draw time.
  2. Switch to the simple statistics view to see the frequency of each number, adjust the number of periods, and use tapping to filter.
  1. By running a local database, past draw records can be quickly retrieved and instantly compared, showing similar records of fourth prize or above.
  2. Each draw record comes with a full statistical analysis. You can switch between draw numbers or include special numbers in the statistics, and the results are instantly clear.
  1. You can freely select the year, month, or range of draws to view detailed statistical results. Additional specialized statistics include the highest prize, the largest number of winners, and the most similar records.
  2. Each range contains plenty of interesting data, inviting you to explore the fascinating combinations created by random numbers.
  1. The system will automatically check your records at draw time and clearly display each winning number along with the corresponding prize.
  2. All data will only be stored on your device. For extra security, you may also choose to back it up to Google Drive. There are only three simple operations: upload, download, and delete.
