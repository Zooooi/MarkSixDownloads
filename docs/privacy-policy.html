Mark 6 Stats - Privacy Policy Last Updated: 2026-04-20 This Privacy Policy applies to the Mark 6 Stats app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. 1. Data Collection & Usage: Upon Google Sign-In, we only collect your name, email, and profile picture. These are used solely for UI personalization and identity verification for Google Drive backups. We NEVER share or sell this data. 2. Google Drive Scope: This app only requests access to "files created by this app" (drive.file scope). Mark 6 Stats is technically restricted from accessing any other files in your Google Drive. 3. Data Security: We do not use server-side storage. All data is processed locally or directly transferred to your Google Drive. 4. Deletion & Revocation: Users can delete data or revoke access at any time via: - In-app "Delete Records" feature. - Manually delete backup files in Google Drive. - Revoke access in Google Account Security settings.
