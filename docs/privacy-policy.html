數據收集與使用 Data Collection & Usage:

當您使用 Google 帳號登入時，我們僅會收集您的電子郵件地址、姓名及個人頭像連結。上述資訊僅用於應用程式內的個人化介面顯示，以及 Google Drive 備份身分驗證。除此之外，我們不會上載或收集任何用戶資訊。
When you sign in with your Google account, we only collect your email address, name, and profile picture URL. This information is used solely for personalized interface display within the application and for identity verification in Google Drive backup. Beyond this, we do not upload or collect any user information.

Google Drive 權限限制 Google Drive Scope:

我們嚴格遵守 Google 的最小權限原則，僅申請存取「由本應用程式建立的文件」之權限。這意味著系統在技術上被隔離，絕對無法觸碰您雲端硬碟中的其他私人檔案。
When you sign in with your Google account, we only collect your email address, name, and profile picture URL. This information is used solely for personalized interface display within the application and for identity verification in Google Drive backup. Beyond this, we do not upload or collect any user information.

數據安全性 Data Security:

本應用程式為每位用戶生成唯一的隨機加密金鑰，以確保本地資料庫與雲端備份數據的安全性。
The application generates a unique, random encryption key for each user to ensure the security and privacy of both the local database and cloud backup data.

數據刪除權利 Data Deletion:

用戶可隨時透過以下方式刪除數據：
  1. 在應用程式內刪除紀錄
  2. 卸載應用程式
  3. 手動刪除 Google Drive 內的備份資料夾
  4. 如需撤銷帳號授權，請至 Google 帳號安全性設定中移除本程式的存取權
Users may delete their data at any time through the following methods:
  1. Delete records within the application
  2. Uninstall the application
  3. Manually delete the backup folder in Google Drive
  4. To revoke account authorization, go to your Google Account security settings and remove this application's access
